Tainster supplies you with an overwhelming amount of diverse porn. There's so much to see on this site that it's difficult to know where to begin. The first thing to do would be to check out all the sites that you can access with your one membership.

There are large thumbnails for each scene and you can also see the rating given by other members - in case you want to take their word for it as a way of determining which ones to watch.

Here is on my one beef with this site. With such a large amount of content, at this point they should strongly consider implementing an advanced search engine. The content tags are great, but if users wanted to look up something specific, they would have to peruse thousands of scenes to find the perfect one that caters to their unique tastes.

In all, you're looking at roughly 5,879 videos. It looks like there are more than that, because many are split up into parts. New movies get added to the website often. It's not every day, but you sometimes get a few updates at a time. There have been 17 new flicks added in the last month alone (a video posted in parts is considered a single video even though its parts can be quite long).

The videos can be streamed and downloaded in MP4 files. Downloading gives you three options. For the older films the highest option looks good, but many of the movies offer a stunning Full HD format. The average scene is 20 minutes long, although some sites have ones that can run for an hour or two (generally these come from the large orgy sites). Some updates will have a logo that says "remaster" and what this means is that they've taken an older scene and revamped it to today's standards. You often get remastered movies and bonus vids on the days without a real update.

In terms of photos, there are sets of pictures to go along with every video, but you sometimes get vid caps instead, so there's around 5,729 pic sets. You'll be happy to know that the images usually look quite good, with many high-res ones and they can be downloaded in Zip files.

There's a section on the site for clips, but these are user-generated. The site offers the ability to create your own and share them with the other members. A single clip can be up to 5 minutes long and there are detailed instructions on how to do this. You can save your clips to a special page and create your own playlist.

There's a bonus section with additional videos that appear to mostly come from third-party sites. You can stream them for free, but if you try to download them, you'll be prompted to join the site they're from. There's a live cam section, but it's really just a whitelabel of Seventeen Live. They say that access is included with your membership and although there are some free recorded shows, you'll need to buy credits in order to go private. There are some DVDs to enjoy too.

Tainster is an amusement park of porn that will delight you for months to come. With very frequent updates, gorgeous babes and lots of high-quality hardcore material, this network is definitely worth joining.
