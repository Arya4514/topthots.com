Update done by Will Peters

Published Aug 18, 2005, updated on Jul 30, 2016

If you're getting hot simply anticipating logging into the members' area, then you sure as hell will be sweating once you enter Twistys. Wow! Talk about stunning international babes! I'd give my right arm for each and every one of them. Fortunately, all I need to do is buy a membership to this site, or to Twistys Network.

With near daily updates alternating between new videos and photos, you certainly won't get sexually frustrated waiting for new content and there's a lot of it. Almost every day Twistys (and TwistysNetwork) introduces new sex kittens to the various sites. The majority of the action is solo, with babes showing off their hot bodies and pleasuring themselves, but select "Twistys Hard" and you'll discover 450 instances of hardcore action.

Since there's so much to see, it's nice to have a variety of navigational options. Use the menu bar on top to isolate the latest updates, just the photos or videos, or both. You can also browse the model index and sort the girls in a few ways. Once you're in the photo or video section, you can use several sorting options such as date, rating and title.

There are thousands of girls filling the model index, so it's nice that they give you an advanced filtering system. You've got sorting options, like by rating, release date and number of scenes. You can also view material from a certain time period, like this week, the last year, or upcoming. Most importantly, there are the model-attribute drop-downs that let you choose from criteria like eye color, cup size and ethnicity.

Now for the bad news. A standard membership no longer includes video downloads, so you'll have to pay extra each month for VIP access if you want to download any of the 8,000 videos. At least you can count on HD streams, or sometimes Full HD for newer ones. That's a lot of movies, but when it comes to pagination, it might look as if you can only move through the pages a few at a time, but there's a drop-down list allowing you to select every fifth page.

There are also 18,415 photo sets that contain between 30 and 135 images that you can download in a Zip file. These usually high-res photos aren't always as sharp as they could be. After looking at a number of sets, I blame this on photographers using a soft-focus lens, because most of the shots are so clear that you can see every raised pore on a babe's luscious skin.

As for exclusivity, noting recognizable pornstar names and producers like Matrix, I know that some of the content is found either on DVDs or elsewhere, but the percentage of nonexclusive material is pretty small. Besides, considering the amount of porn and quality, I'd be willing to overlook it.

In the end, I've viewed enough sites to say with certainty that Twistys has one of the largest collections of hot babes out there, so the fact that downloads are no longer are included with a standard membership shouldn't discourage you from joining, as its high-quality streaming footage is still worth checking out.
