Pros and Cons

  • many high-def movies
  • frequent updates across the network
  • lots of usability features
  • can't post comments
  • no advanced search engine
  • limited trial

What's the first thing that comes to mind when I say Hustler? If it's the classic movie about pool players starring Paul Newman then I say good answer, but wrong! If instead you think of a magazine that's been a leader in the hardcore world for decades then I say right on!

This super-site encompasses porn from nearly 20 sites (including Hustler itself) and there are many niches explored throughout the network. If we take a look at some of them you've got Asians with Asian Fever, hot anal on Anal Hookers, big boobs on Busty Beauties and sexy amateur hotties on Beaver Hunt.

The site is well organized and looks good. When you log in you'll be greeted by a giant picture of the featured babe/site/scene of the month. Below that you'll find all the latest additions including videos, photos, models and magazines. In order to navigate to the different sites, you'll have to click on the "all sites" tab.

The videos can be sorted in a variety of ways (latest updates, most viewed, title, top rated, etc.). I was surprised to find that there's still no advanced search engine, which would be useful when you consider how large the site is. Even so, the content tags work network-wide and you can use as few or as many as you want. The category section is also a great help for looking up your preferred niche and, luckily, you can keep track of all your favorites. You can leave ratings, but not comments.

At the moment, there are more than 12,177 movies. You get HD footage from the best of the flicks, but the rest aren't as high in quality - they still look good, though. You can download them in MP4 files or a mix of MP4 and WMV files and stream them all in an embedded Flash player, but keep in mind that some movies may resize larger than others in the embedded player.

I counted 4,613 photo galleries this visit. The images look good and are high-res when you download them in the Zip files. The photos don't always match the videos and they sometimes give you a mix of posing and stripping, while others bring you digital versions of Hustler's printed magazines.

While not all the sites are updating, many continue to post new content, so you can expect to get new material every few days (several vids can be posted at the same time). There's also an archive of Hustler magazines that dates back to 2007 and a link to Hustler live cams. You also get deals to other sites and you can keep up with this porn powerhouse through their social media feeds. Overall I feel very good about recommending this network.
